# the-riddle-bridge-2
A pixel art riddle game — answer 10 riddles to cross a lava bridge or fall forever.
A dark pixel art browser game built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

You stand before a broken bridge over a river of lava.
The only way across? Answer 10 riddles correctly.

## How to Play
- A riddle appears on screen — type your answer and press ENTER
- Answer correctly → a tile appears and your character steps forward
- Stuck? Sacrifice one of your 5 lights to skip the riddle
- Run out of lights or answer wrong → your character falls into the lava

## Features
- 10 original riddles with increasing difficulty
- 5 sacrifice lights — use them wisely
- Pixel art character with walk and fall animations
- Neon gothic visual theme with glowing effects
- Lava death screen and multiple win endings based on performance
